How a run works

Your current public ratings are fetched live, compared against every user in the dataset pool (seconds, offline), and then the top-ranked matches are re-verified against their current public ratings — dropping accounts that have since gone private or changed taste. Verification is the slow part: it runs at a strict 1 request/second with an identifying User-Agent, honors robots.txt and rate-limit headers, and caches every page for a week so re-runs cost almost nothing.

Data

The candidate pool is the CC0 Kaggle dataset freeth/letterboxd-film-ratings (~11,000 of Letterboxd's most active members, ~18.2M ratings, October 2023 snapshot). On first boot the app downloads it once (~600 MB) and builds a local SQLite index. If the automatic download fails, place ratings.csv and films.csv in data/dataset/ and re-run.

This is a personal project and is not affiliated with Letterboxd.
